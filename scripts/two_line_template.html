How to use this: Add Line 1 (locked) and Line 2 (pass-through) separately in every board report and every homeowner communication — never as one combined "total bill" number. If your Line 2 total has grown faster than your Line 1 total, that is the clearest sign a documented 12-month operating baseline and reserve-study review would help your board before your next renewal.
